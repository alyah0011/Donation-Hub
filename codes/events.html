Walk For Life

CanSupport Walk for Life is an annual walkathon that is held every year on the first Sunday of February to celebrate World Cancer Day. Since 2008, CanSupport has been holding this event to create awareness about the rising incidence of cancer, show solidarity with cancer patients and their caregivers and raise funds to take care of them.

Annual Leukemia Ball

We look forward to coming back together with our supporters at this safe, in-person event at the Marriott Marquis! Come laugh, dance and celebrate 35 years of advancements toward an end to blood cancer.
