Donation Hub is an organization which focuses on helping to improve the lives of the poor and needy people in our community. This website is especially made for the generous and kind-hearted people to donate some of their happiness to those who have less. In this website, every donated items will be listed along with where or which events it was donated to. Furthermore, we encourage people who are interested to join our volunteering programme.

The Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs) Agenda which was adopted by the United Nation in 2015 is a blueprint to achieve a better and more sustainable future for all without exclusion which is intended to be accomplished in 15 years. In line with the global goals, we hope for this website to make a positive impact on as many Sustainable Development Goals as possible such as No poverty, Zero Hunger and Good Health and Well-being with the motto of “Spread Hands for Love”.
