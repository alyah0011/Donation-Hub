Walk For Life

Date: Sunday, 6 February 2022

Venue: International Islamic University Malaysia

CanSupport Walk for Life is an annual walkathon that is held every year on the first Sunday of February to celebrate World Cancer Day. Since 2008, CanSupport has been holding this event to create awareness about the rising incidence of cancer, show solidarity with cancer patients and their caregivers and raise funds to take care of them.

Every year, people from all walks of life, different age groups and professions, walk or run together for one common cause to fight against cancer, to cheer and support cancer patients and their caregivers.

Keeping in mind the ongoing coronavirus pandemic and social distancing norms, you can join the event on Sunday, 6 February 2022, from wherever you are.

Schools, colleges, RWAs, corporates and other groups and institutions can also organise small group walks inside their premises or in a park near them.
