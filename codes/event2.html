Annual Leukemia Ball

Date: Saturday, 18 June 2022

Venue: Marriott Marquis

We look forward to coming back together with our supporters at this safe, in-person event at the Marriott Marquis! Come laugh, dance and celebrate 35 years of advancements toward an end to blood cancer. Guests will enjoy a world-class live and silent auction. Our 2022 headlining comedic entertainer is Roy Wood Jr., correspondent on Comedy Central’s Emmy-nominated The Daily Show with Trevor Noah. Headline musical entertainment will be brought to you by The Legwarmers, Washington, D.C.’s Ultimate 80s Tribute Band. Thank you to our Comedy Sponsor, PwC, and our Music Sponsor, BDO. Sponsorships and individual ticket sales are now available.

Our event begins with a General Reception at 6:30pm, followed by Awards, Live Auction, and Dinner. Shortly after 9:00pm is our Mission Moment, Fund the Fight, Comedic Entertainment Presented by PwC, and Musical Entertainment Presented by BDO. The event concludes by 12:00am.

To protect the safety of guests and our industry, all guests, sponsors, and individuals (age 12+) working or attending the event are required to show proof that they have received two doses of the COVID-19 vaccine (5) days prior to the event. Patrons also have the option to show proof of a negative COVID test taken within 24 hours of the event. Information on steps around this verification will be communicated to guests closer to the date of event. Masking will be optional to all guests, but encouraged, especially for those immunocompromised.
